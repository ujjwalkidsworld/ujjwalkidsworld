Frequently Asked Questions

Why Our School?

Ujjwal Kids World is not just a school, it's a nurturing environment where children are encouraged to explore, learn, and grow.

What Is hapening in Ujjwal Kids World?

At Ujjwal Kids World, every day is filled with exciting and enriching experiences for our students. From innovative learning activities to fun-filled events, there's always something happening in our vibrant school community.

What Is our Vision ?

At Ujjwal Kids World, our vision is to create a nurturing and dynamic learning environment where every child is inspired to excel academically, emotionally, and socially. We envision a school where creativity is celebrated, curiosity is nurtured, and diversity is embraced.

What Is Our Mission

At Ujjwal Kids World, our mission is to provide a stimulating and inclusive learning environment where every child is valued, respected, and empowered to reach their full potential.

What is our Facilities?

At Ujjwal Kids World, we pride ourselves on providing a safe, stimulating, and well-equipped environment that fosters learning, creativity, and exploration.

What Is Our Skill Development?

At Ujjwal Kids World, we foster academic excellence, communication proficiency, and creativity. Our holistic approach includes teaching coding skills, promoting social-emotional learning, and nurturing leadership development, preparing students for success in a rapidly evolving world.
