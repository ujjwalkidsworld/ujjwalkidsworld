About Us

Welcome to Ujjwal Kids World, where education meets inspiration and every child's potential is nurtured to shine brightly. Founded with a vision to redefine traditional schooling, Ujjwal Kids World is a beacon of excellence in Gangarampur and beyond. Our holistic approach to education emphasizes academic excellence, creativity, and character development, preparing students for success in a rapidly changing world. At Ujjwal Kids World, we believe in empowering students to become lifelong learners, critical thinkers, and compassionate leaders. Our dedicated faculty members are committed to providing a supportive and enriching learning environment where every child can thrive. With innovative teaching methods, cutting-edge technology, and a focus on holistic development, we strive to unlock each child's unique talents and abilities. From our pioneering coding curriculum to our dynamic STEAM projects and cultural exchange programs, Ujjwal Kids World offers a diverse range of opportunities for students to explore their interests, discover their passions, and reach their full potential. Our commitment to excellence extends beyond the classroom, as we actively engage with the community and instill values of social responsibility and global citizenship in our students. As we continue our journey of growth and innovation, we invite you to join us at Ujjwal Kids World and experience the difference that inspired education can make. Together, let's create a brighter future for our children and empower them to become the leaders and innovators of tomorrow. Welcome to Ujjwal Kids World, where every child's dreams take flight!
